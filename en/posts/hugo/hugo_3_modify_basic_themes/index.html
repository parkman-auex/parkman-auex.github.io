Hugo 3 Modify Basic Themes

Sep 1, 2021 1 min read HugoHugo

See https://themes.gohugo.io/themes/hugo-theme-bootstrap/ .

modify information

modify author

enter the configure directory.
cd config/_default
configure information of author in the author.toml
subl author.toml

modify external link for socialize

subl social.toml

modify copyright

1cd ../produciton
2cp _default/config.toml .
3subl config.toml

modify parameters

1cp _default/params.toml .
2subl params.toml

2keywords = ""
